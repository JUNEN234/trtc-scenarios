Overview

Virtual Try-On Makeup is a mobile shopping feature that lets customers see how makeup products look on their face in real time using AR. They can try lipsticks, foundations, eyeshadows, and blush through the camera, compare before/after, and purchase directly. Beauty consultants can join video calls to provide personalized advice.

Why Tencent RTC? TRTC's real-time video pipeline supports custom video processing plugins, allowing AR face mesh overlays to be applied before encoding. Combined with Tencent's AI face detection SDK, you get 128-point face landmarks at 30fps.

Key features

  • Real-time AR try-on — lipstick, foundation, eyeshadow, blush applied on live camera feed at 30fps.
  • 128-point face mesh — precise landmark detection for natural-looking makeup placement.
  • Before/After toggle — swipe or tap to compare with and without makeup.
  • Product catalog integration — tap a shade to instantly apply; buy button leads to checkout.
  • 1-on-1 beauty consultation — video call with consultant who can see your AR try-on in real time.
  • Photo capture & share — save try-on looks and share to social media.
