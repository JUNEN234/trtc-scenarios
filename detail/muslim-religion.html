Overview

Religious Community is a mobile app for Muslim communities to gather virtually for prayer sessions, Quran study groups, Friday sermons (Khutbah), and Islamic lectures. It combines scheduled live audio/video broadcasts, private study rooms, prayer time alerts, and Quran recitation features.

Why Tencent RTC? TRTC's audio room mode delivers crystal-clear recitation with minimal bandwidth, while conference mode supports study groups with up to 50 active participants. Scheduled room features and server-side recording let communities replay sessions.

Key features

  • Scheduled prayer rooms — auto-open rooms at prayer times (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha) with adhan playback.
  • Group Quran study — up to 50 participants, teacher controls who reads, real-time verse highlighting.
  • Friday Khutbah broadcast — one-to-many livestream with multi-language audio tracks.
  • Private audio rooms — small study circles with hand-raise moderation.
  • Prayer time notifications — location-based, accurate prayer times with push alerts.
  • Cloud recording — all sessions recorded and available for replay.
