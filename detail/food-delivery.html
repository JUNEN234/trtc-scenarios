Overview

Food Delivery Chat & Call adds real-time communication between diners, delivery riders, and restaurants within a food delivery app. Privacy-masked calls hide real phone numbers, in-order chat keeps context tied to each delivery, and location sharing helps riders find drop-off points.

Why Tencent RTC? TRTC's number-privacy voice calls let riders and diners talk without exposing personal phone numbers. The call is auto-scoped to the active order and torn down when delivery completes.

Key features

  • Privacy-masked voice calls — riders and diners call each other without seeing real numbers.
  • Order-scoped chat — messages tied to delivery orders, auto-archived on completion.
  • Quick reply templates — 'I'm at the gate', 'Leave at door', 'Running 5 min late'.
  • Live location sharing — rider's real-time position on the map.
  • Auto-translation — for cross-language rider-diner communication.
  • Call recording — optional recording for dispute resolution.
