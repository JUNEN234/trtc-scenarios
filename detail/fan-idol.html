Overview

Fan & Idol Live is a mobile-first live-streaming platform where idols broadcast to millions of fans in real time. Fans interact through gifts, chat, lottery draws, and can be invited on-stage for co-hosting video calls. The platform needs sub-second latency for the live feed, a scalable gift animation engine, real-time chat moderation, and a revenue system for virtual gifts.

Why Tencent RTC? Tencent TRTC's CDN relay + co-hosting architecture delivers < 300ms latency for on-stage guests while serving millions of viewers through CDN with < 1s latency. Built-in beauty filters and mixing keep the production quality high.

Key features

  • Mega-scale livestream — single room supports 1M+ concurrent viewers via TRTC CDN relay.
  • Co-hosting — fans join stage as video guests with < 300ms latency, seamless mic/camera toggling.
  • Virtual gifting engine — animated gifts (Lottie), combo multipliers, real-time revenue dashboard.
  • Live lottery & red packets — timed draws during stream with fair randomization.
  • Real-time chat — Tencent IM integration with spam filter, slow mode, and pinned messages.
  • Beauty filters — TRTC built-in face beautification, stickers, and virtual backgrounds.
