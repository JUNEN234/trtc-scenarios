Overview

In-Chat Red Packet adds a lucky money (hongbao) feature to any chat app. Users can send red packets in 1-on-1 or group conversations. Group red packets support "lucky draw" (random split) and "equal split" modes. The feature needs atomic fund transfers, real-time claim notifications, and expiry refunds.

Why Tencent RTC? While red packets are primarily a chat feature (powered by Tencent IM), TRTC integrates with IM for the signaling layer — enabling red packet rain during livestreams and video calls as an engagement mechanic.

Key features

  • 1-on-1 red packets — send fixed-amount hongbao in private chats.
  • Group lucky draw — random split among N claimants (WeChat-style).
  • Equal split — fixed amount per person in group.
  • Expiry & refund — unclaimed red packets auto-refund after 24 hours.
  • Claim animation — gold coin burst animation on open.
  • Red packet history — sent/received ledger with totals.
