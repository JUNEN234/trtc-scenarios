Overview

Ride-Hailing Chat & Call adds privacy-protected communication between passengers and drivers in a ride-hailing app. All calls are anonymous (masked numbers), chat is scoped to the active trip, and the system automatically manages the communication lifecycle from dispatch to drop-off.

Why Tencent RTC? TRTC handles anonymous voice calls without exposing phone numbers, with automatic room teardown when trips end. Signaling through IM keeps chat lightweight and trip-scoped.

Key features

  • Anonymous voice calls — passenger and driver talk through TRTC without revealing phone numbers.
  • Trip-scoped chat — messages auto-create on dispatch, auto-archive on trip end.
  • Predefined messages — 'I'm at the pin', 'Red car', 'Running late' — one-tap send.
  • Live location sharing — driver position + ETA updates on the map.
  • Safety recording — all calls recorded for safety; accessible to support team only.
  • Multi-language support — auto-translate between passenger and driver languages.
