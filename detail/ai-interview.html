Overview

AI Video Interview is a recruitment platform where candidates are interviewed by an AI-powered virtual recruiter through video call. The AI asks adaptive questions, provides real-time transcription, scores sentiment and communication skills, and generates structured feedback reports for hiring managers.

Why Tencent RTC? TRTC's server-side push stream lets the AI 'sit' in the call as a virtual participant with a generated avatar. Combined with streaming ASR and TTS, the AI can conduct natural, low-latency conversations.

Key features

  • AI interviewer — conversational agent that asks role-specific questions and follow-ups.
  • Real-time transcription — live captions for both AI and candidate speech.
  • Sentiment analysis — tracks confidence, nervousness, and enthusiasm throughout.
  • Adaptive questioning — follow-up questions based on candidate's answers.
  • Structured feedback report — auto-generated scorecard for hiring managers.
  • Multi-language support — interview in English, Mandarin, Spanish, Arabic, and more.
