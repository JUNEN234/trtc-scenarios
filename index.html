FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before integrating Tencent RTC into your product.

How long does it take to integrate Tencent RTC into my app? +
Most teams ship a working prototype in under 30 minutes using our UIKit components (TUICallKit, TUILiveKit, TUIRoomKit). A full production integration with custom UI, authentication, and server-side signing typically takes 1–3 days. Our SDK supports iOS, Android, Web, Flutter, React Native, Electron, and Unity out of the box.
Do I need to run my own media servers? +
No. Tencent RTC is a fully managed global real-time network with 3,200+ acceleration nodes across 200+ countries and regions. We handle routing, transcoding, recording, and failover. You only need to generate short-lived UserSig tokens on your backend (we provide server SDKs for Node.js, Go, Java, Python, PHP, and C#).
What's the pricing model? Is there a free tier? +
Yes — every account gets 10,000 free minutes per month across audio, video, and live streaming. Beyond that, you pay only for what you use: starting at $0.99 per 1,000 audio minutes and $3.99 per 1,000 HD video minutes. Volume discounts kick in automatically past 1M minutes. No seat fees, no setup cost.
How do you handle latency and quality on poor networks? +
Our global median end-to-end latency is under 300ms. We use adaptive bitrate (SVC), packet-loss concealment, AI-based bandwidth estimation, and FEC to maintain call quality even at 70% packet loss. The SDK automatically switches between UDP, TCP, and QUIC transports based on network conditions.
Is Tencent RTC secure and compliant for regulated industries? +
All media streams are encrypted in transit with DTLS-SRTP (AES-128). For regulated workloads, we offer optional end-to-end encryption (E2EE), customer-managed keys, and data residency in EU, US, SG, and JP regions. Tencent Cloud is certified for ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI DSS.
